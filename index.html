I am Assistant Professor in the Department of Philosophy at the University of Massachusetts, Amherst. I work mostly in the philosophy of mind and language, metaphysics, and the philosophy of mathematics. I am also interested in some questions in metaethics, formal epistemology, and the philosophy of science.


I received my PhD from the Department of Linguistics and Philosophy at MIT in 2011.
